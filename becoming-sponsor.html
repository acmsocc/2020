Becoming a sponsor

The ACM Symposium on Cloud Computing is the premier research conference on cloud computing. Attendees include top graduate students and premier experts from academia and industry. The 2019 conference will be held in Chaminade, Santa Cruz, California, and attracts a variety of attendees from industry and universities.

We welcome sponsorship! Sponsoring the conference is an excellent way to attract potential recruits, get visibility for your company and products, and be a part of the cloud computing research community.
