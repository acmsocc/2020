Funding for child-care travel expenses

The SoCC 2019 planning committee with the support of Microsoft, is pleased to offer funding support for conference attendees travelling with infants/young children towards any related expenses. The expected support is $400 per applicant. Since the funding is limited, priority will be given to applicants travelling with infants. Applicants should fill in the childcare support form.
