Student Package

Full time students are eligible for the student package, which includes the conference registration, lodging in a shared room for three nights (we will help with roommate assignments), all meals, all taxes and all fees.

  • Early Registration: $875 ($475 for the conference, charged at registration, and $400 lodging charged at checkout)

  • Late Registration: $1,075 ($675 for the conference, charged at registration, and $400 lodging charged at checkout)

ACM/SIG Member Packages

Student scholarships

Thanks to generous donations from SIGMOD, SIGOPS, and NSF, SoCC'18 will offer scholarships to assist students attending the conference.
Visit the student scholarship page for more information about how to apply. Deadline is September 1.

Diversity Grants

As part of our ongoing commitment to encourage diversity in advanced computing, we are pleased to offer Diversity Grants to support computer scientists interested in attending SoCC'18. All computer scientists from academia or industry who identify as female or a member of another underrepresented community are encouraged to apply. Awardees will receive funds to apply toward travel and/or accomodation costs (including child care) at the conference. These Grants are offered thanks to generous support from SIGOPS and NSF.
Visit the diversity grants page for more information about how to apply. Deadline is September 4.
